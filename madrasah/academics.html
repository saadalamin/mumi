Curriculum

In this Madrasa, the curriculum follows the approved and accredited syllabus from the Bangladesh Madrasa Education Board, Dhaka, covering from the primary level to the secondary level.

At every level, the curriculum includes the following subjects:

  • Bangladesh Madrasa Education Board, Dhaka's approved curriculum (from first grade to Dakhil).
Extra Curricular Activities:
  • Cultural Competition
  • Annual Sports Competition
  • Handwriting Competition
  • Wall Magazine Competition
  • Annual Educational Tour
  • Training in Hamd-Naat
